Fully Anticipated Questions (FAQs)

If your question is not answered, just send us an email through the contact page.

  • Where can I find updates?
    expand_more

    Make sure you are in the appropriate Google Classroom; if not, contact one of our sponsors (Ms. Gillevet at ezgillevet1@fcps.edu or Ms. Nelson at lhnelson@fcps.edu) for the code. Also, join our Facebook group once you are admitted, check your email, and keep track of Ion posts. If you haven’t noticed any posts in a while, you may want to reach out to the officers.

  • If you are fundraising for a charity, is that service, or no?
    expand_more

    Fundraising/donating on their own do not count. Organizing a fundraiser may be applicable, though, if you don’t receive any incentives and you have clearly outlined hours that can be confirmed by an adult sponsor.

  • Does tutoring count as service?
    expand_more

    Yes, as long as you are not getting paid/receiving an incentive, and have an adult sponsor.

  • What are exclusive/non-exclusive hours?
    expand_more

    NHS is NOT using x2VOL this year.

  • Would hours completed over the summer count towards the 6 required?
    expand_more

    Yes, as long as it was after the 2019-20 school year ended (after June 13, 2020).

  • Can we count service hours that are already being counted towards the service learning cord and civics seal for NHS?
    expand_more

    Submit hours for NHS in Google Classroom and any additional hours into x2VOL to apply to other goals/seals outside of NHS.

  • Would tutoring at the Writing Center count as service?
    expand_more

    Yes, as long as you are counting hours where you are actively helping someone.

  • What do we do as a club?
    expand_more

    The main requirement for members of the club is to meet service hours deadlines. This year circumstances are different, but we will try to keep everyone updated on service opportunities, and also host opportunities.

  • What if my online service does not have an adult sponsor to sign off on my hours? (2020-21 school year)
    expand_more

    This year given the circumstances we are allowing parents to sign off on hours, so put them as your contact if necessary.

  • Did we have to get the hours during the summer? What is the timeframe?
    expand_more

    You do not need to do hours over the summer, but if you do it will count towards your first semester requirement. Six hours from June 15th (end of the 2019-20 school year) to January 22nd (end of first semester). As a member, you will need another 12 hours from Jan 22nd to the end of the 2020-21 school year.

  • Is there anywhere specific where we need to log our hours?
    expand_more

    This year, NHS is not using x2Vol to log hours. Please submit your hours through Google Classroom only; if you have hours additional to the requirement, you may log them in x2Vol to apply to other goals outside of NHS.

  • What is the name of your facebook page?
    expand_more

    Most announcements are posted in our private Facebook group (TJ National Honor Society 2020-21), though, so join the group once you’re admitted.

  • How do I become part of NHS leadership?
    expand_more

    If you are a junior, you may apply to become a Junior Chair (3 Service Chairs, 1 Communications Chair, 1 Technology Chair) in February-March. In May or June elections will be held for President, Vice-President, and Secretary-Treasurer.

Application Process

  • Is the application rolling or does it happen at a set time?
    expand_more

    Applications to NHS happen at a set time. The application season typically opens with an interest meeting in October available during 8th period which details the application process. Applications are normally due in early November.

  • What are the requirements for applying?
    expand_more

    You must be a junior or a senior and meet a minimum GPA of 3.5. You must also have earned 6 hours of service since the start of the summer upon applying.

  • Can I apply in senior year?
    expand_more

    Yes :)

  • Is it an invitation or an application?
    expand_more

    You fill out the application to potentially get an invitation to be accepted into NHS.

  • On our applications can we put if we are an officer of a school club even if we were appointed at the end of last year/start of this year?
    expand_more

    Yes, just remember to put an X in the grade that you held/made a significant impact as that position.

  • Is it pretty difficult to get in?
    expand_more

    No; we are an open community, and the application process is not meant to be punitive. Just make sure to put an honest effort into your application and essays--your work will be viewed by faculty members who are taking time out of their days to grade applications. And turn in your forms in time! There are only two (the applicant and parent forms), and failure to do so will result in your application being rejected.

  • What if we are struggling to find in-person opportunities?
    expand_more

    We have noticed a large number of volunteer organizations that have adapted to an online format, but if you are having problems finding somewhere to volunteer, please reach out to us and we’ll try to connect you to opportunities.

  • What should I write for the description of my service in my hours spreadsheet? (2020-21 school year)
    expand_more

    One or two sentences describing what you specifically did is sufficient.

  • Is there a word limit to the essays?
    expand_more

    Yes, but it is not a hard and fast requirement; it is okay to go 5-10 words over the word limit.

  • Why is there such a focus on having been a leader in a club/extracurricular activity?
    expand_more

    One of our four pillars is founded on leadership and we love to see active participation of our members in clubs they are passionate in. A leadership position doesn't necessarily have to be an officer position, but anytime you helped or contributed to a group over a significant period of time.

  • Does NHS leadership review the application?
    expand_more

    No, applications will be reviewed by faculty members from TJ.

  • Why might my application have been denied?
    expand_more

    You may have failed to submit your application or parent form; just filling it out is not enough, you must press “Turn In”!

  • Why might my membership have been revoked?
    expand_more

    You may have failed to submit your hours; just filling out your spreadsheet is not enough, you must press “Turn In” and fill out the Google Form!
